How to enable or disable usb ports in windows

To disable access to USB port

Click "Start" then click "Run".
type "regedit" to open Registry Editor, and then click "OK".
Search for the following registry key:

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Services\UsbStor

At your right, double-click "Start".
In the Value data box, type 4, click Hexadecimal, and then click "OK".
Close Registry Editor and Restart the machine.

 To enable access to USB port

To reverse this process and enable USB, follow the above instructions but change the 4 to 3.
